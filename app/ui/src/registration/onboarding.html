Onboarding steps

  1. Register your company
  2. Prepare eID software, reader and card
    1. Install European eID software for Windows or MacOS or Linux (alternatively you can install the full software stack of id.ee)
    2. Make sure the Belgian eID software BeIDConnect is also installed
    3. Restart the browser and make sure Web eID extension is enabled (more information for Chrome or Firefox or Safari or Microsoft Edge )
    4. Connect eID reader with eID card and use the test below
    5. Confirm your identity in Web eID, if it does not work :
      • Try again by removing the eID card and sliding it in again
      • Try direct USB connection (minimize USB dongles)
      • Try full restart
      • Try different computer
      • Contact us on support@letspeppol.org
  3. Click the email verification link in your email, check your spam folder for emails from @letspeppol.org
  4. Verify your identity as director of the company
  5. Sign the contract as part of the KYC (Know-Your-Customer) obligations
  6. Login to your account
