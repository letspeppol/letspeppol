Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. Like Let's Encrypt, Let's Peppol is free to use. The project is funded by donations and sponsors. If you find it valuable, please consider supporting us.

What about uptime and support?

We aim for high availability and quick community support, but uptime commitments are not legally binding. Formal support/SLA details are in the KYC contract upon onboarding.

Do I own my data?

Always. We do not sell, mine, or repurpose your business data.

Which languages are supported?

English, Dutch, and French at launch (German planned). Support us by adding additional languages.
