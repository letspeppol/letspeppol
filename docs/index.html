Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. Like Let's Encrypt, Let's Peppol is free to use. The project is funded by donations and sponsors. If you find it valuable, please consider supporting us.

Do I need my Belgian eID and reader every time?

No. You only need your eID card + reader during registration. After onboarding, it’s a normal, simple login for everyday use.

What do I need to install for Belgian registration?

You’ll need Web-eID (European eID software) and the Belgian eID software. Once registration is done, you don’t need the reader for regular use.

What about uptime and support?

We aim for high availability and quick community support, but uptime commitments are not legally binding. Formal support/SLA details are in the contract upon onboarding.

Do I own my data?

Always. We do not sell, mine, or repurpose your business data.

Which languages are supported?

English, Dutch, and French at launch (German planned). Support us by adding additional languages.
